General Grievous

Grievous is a character in the Star Wars universe. He is a Sith Lord and the leader of the Separatist movement during the Clone Wars. He is known for his red lightsaber and his unique voice.

Anakin Skywalker

Anakin Skywalker is a character in the Star Wars universe. He is a Jedi Knight and he eventually becomes Darth Vader at the end of Revenge of the Sith.

Anakin Skywalker is also known for the change that he has made since his childhood. Where he was once a great Jedi Knight he then becomes one of the most feared villains in the Star Wars universe.

Anakin Skywalker is in love with Padme Amidala.

Obi-Wan Kenobi

Obi Wan Kenobi is a Jedi Knight, who is a mentor to Anakin Skywalker. He is also a great Jedi Knight who is known for his wisdom and his ability to use the Force.

Padme Amidala

Padme is the queen of Naboo and a great leader. She is also the main love interest of Anakin Skywalker and the mother of Luke Skywalker.

Padme's death is one of the most tragic moments in the Star Wars universe, and ultimately the reason for the death of Anakin Skywalker and the creation of Darth Vader.

Darth Maul

Darth Maul is a great Sith Lord and a great fighter, known for wielding a double bladed lightsaber.

In the Episode I - The Phantom Menace, he is the apprentice of Darth Sidious and at the end of the movie he kills Qui-Gon Jinn and then is killed by Obi-Wan Kenobi shortly after.

Mace Windu

Mace Windu is a Jedi Master, and a respected member of the Jedi Council.

Darth Sidious

Darth Sidious is the master of the Sith and the leader of the Separatist movement during the Clone Wars.

He plays a key part in the downfall of the Jedi Order and the rise of the Sith, and is the main antagonist of the Star Wars prequel trilogy.

Through careful manipulation, he turns Anakin Skywalker to the dark side to then become Darth Vader.

Plo Koon

Plo Koon has a blue lightsaber and is a Jedi Knight.

In Episode IV - A New Hope, he destroys the Death Star.

Darth Vader

Darth Vader is a powerful Sith Lord.

Darth Vader was once a Jedi Knight - Anakin Skywalker, but was turned to the dark side by Darth Sidious.

Darth Tyranus (Count Dooku)

Darth Tyranus is a powerful Sith Lord and a great fighter.

He was killed by Anakin Skywalker in Revenge of the Sith after being instructed to by the Chancellor.

Luke Skywalker

Luke Skywalker is a Jedi Knight and the son of Anakin Skywalker and Padme Amidala.

Luke Skywalker is the main protagonist of the Star Wars original trilogy.

Jabba the Hutt

Jabba the Hutt is an infamous crime lord and the leader of the Hutt crime family.

He is featured in Episode VI - Return of the Jedi, where is holds Han Solo and Leia Organa hostage.

Kylo Ren

Kylo Ren is the main antagonist of the Star Wars sequel trilogy.

He is the son of Han Solo and Leia Organa and he becomes a great Sith Lord.

Storm Trooper

Storm Troopers are the main soldiers of the Galactic Empire.

They are known for their white armor and their blaster rifles.

Imperial Guard

The Imperial Guard can usually be seen accompanying Darth Sidious.

They are distinctive from other soldiers by their bright red armor.

Rey Skywalker

Rey Skywalker is the main protagonist of the Star Wars sequel trilogy.

Rey Skywalker is brought up by Miramir and Dathan and is the daughter of Luke Skywalker.
